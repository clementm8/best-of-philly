Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)

For the Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup Alfredo sauce (for a rich, creamy base that lets the steak and cheese shine)

Toppings:

  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup provolone cheese, shredded (a must for that authentic Philly taste!)
  • 1/2 lb ribeye steak, thinly sliced (the more tender, the better)
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • Olive oil (for cooking the steak)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Optional Finishes:

  • Fresh chopped parsley for garnish
  • Crushed red pepper flakes for a little extra kick
